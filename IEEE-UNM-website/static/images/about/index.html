About Us

  • IEEE UNM SB stands for the Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineering University of Nottingham Student Branch.
  • We are an academic society that organises various events and activities throughout the academic terms in the University of Nottingham, Malaysia.

Aim

Aligned with the mission of IEEE, IEEE UNM SB aims to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Objectives

  • To provide a networking platform for electrical and electronics engineers.
  • To nurture the mindset of professionalism and innovate thinking.
  • To sharpen the technical skills and soft skills of members to better prepare them for the industry.
  • To nurture volunteerism to support impactful humanitarian activities on a community or local level.
  • To promote the international organization IEEE.

Vision

To create a rapport community that fosters great teamwork and inspires lifelong-learning in the field of Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

Mission

IEEE UNM SB organises participant-centered events and activities that will benefit the participants in their professional and personal development.
